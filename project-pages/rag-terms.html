RAG for Terms & Services
A tool designed to help users understand legal terms through smart retrieval and clear, AI-generated responses.
Project Details / Background
Terms and conditions are everywhereâ€”but hardly anyone reads them. This project was about changing that. I built a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system that lets users ask freeform questions about complex legal texts and get clear, reliable answers.
The project involved creating multiple chunking strategies, comparing embedding models, and experimenting with vector search in Pinecone. I also designed a front-end using Streamlit to make the system intuitive and transparent. Beyond the tech, it was about giving people clarity in a space that often feels deliberately confusing.
